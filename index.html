Our Services

At Better Shine Enterprise we deliver professional metal finishing services that combine durability, corrosion protection and fine aesthetics. Below each service you’ll find a demo of our work.

Electro-Galvanizing

Thin, uniform zinc coating applied via electroplating — excellent for small parts and fasteners.

Uniform zinc layer for even protection
Quick turnaround for small batches
Cleaner finish suited to fasteners & electronics

Cold Galvanizing

Protective zinc-rich coating applied at room temperature ideal for touch-ups and maintenance on galvanized surfaces.

Ideal for repairing or touching up damaged galvanized surfaces
Provides long-lasting corrosion resistance

Hot-Dip Galvanizing

Thick zinc coating from immersion in molten zinc ideal for heavy-duty and outdoor applications.

Thick, rugged zinc layer for durability
Perfect for structural and outdoor components
Exceptional long-term corrosion protection

Chroming

High-gloss chrome plating for decorative appeal and added corrosion resistance.

Mirror-like decorative finish
Great for trims, accessories & parts
Extra resistance to wear and corrosion

Engraving

Precision engraving for identification, traceability and branding on plates and components.

Accurate serial numbers & logos
Works on metals, plates and tags
Durable, legible marks for traceability

Polishing

Professional polishing to create smooth, reflective surfaces that enhance appearance and longevity.

Smooth, high-gloss finishes
Removes minor defects & improves aesthetics
Protects surfaces and extends life

Electroplating

Electrochemical metal coatings for improved corrosion resistance, wear resistance or decorative finishes.

Precise metal deposition control
Custom finishes to spec
Functional and decorative improvements

Buffing

Mechanical polishing to remove scratches and produce a mirror-like finish on metal surfaces.

Removes tiny surface imperfections
Achieves lustrous, reflective finish
Enhances overall appearance

Galvanizing

Protective zinc coating on steel/iron to prevent rust — suitable for structural and outdoor components.

Full-coverage protective zinc layers
Great for heavy structures & frameworks
Superior corrosion resistance outdoors
